Gaming SMU GPA System

1) SMU-X Mods have ~50% A-band, other mods have ~35% A-band

2) Focus on components that indiv Prof's bellcurve, not the components that have the highest weightage. E.g. 20% Class Part could be way less important than that 10% Indiv Assignment if the Prof is going to give 90% of the class A- in Class Part anyway.

3) Go for Profs you can get along with, and drop them otherwise. Be their friend, find out about their lives etc. You don't need to be a class part slut if the profs like you enough

4) You ONLY need 21 graded mods for Single Major in Biz - thereby take mods you are "strong at", map the rest to SUSEP/ISEP/Pass Fail Mods.

5) Don't be overly zealous for group projects. Learn to influence people to do the parts they like, and so long as there's a coherent idea behind each project, you will do fine.

6) Stop worrying too much about exams. Go in there with the sole mentality that you have tried your best (and genuinely just try), and don't stress about the outcome. There is a reason various Y4s have their best sems when they stop studying so hard.

7) Exam techniques. Skip questions you can't do and move on, come back when you can.

8) Make friends everywhere. A friend who understands how you think can be a far better teacher than a Prof/TA. This also just means being nice to people.

9) You can actually get an A-band grade with a B-/C+ in component grades. So even if you mess up once, focus on the next graded component.

But Most Importantly
- Go have fun, live a little, and find yourself in SMU.
